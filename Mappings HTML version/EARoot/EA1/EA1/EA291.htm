A role of an agent that awards the contract. <br/><br/>Additional information:<br/>Examples of a buyer can be the role of contracting authority, contracting entity, a defence contractor, an international organisation, or an organisation awarding a contract subsidized by a contracting authority.<br/><br/><br/>WG Approval  28/04/2020<br/>

Notes: Role of entities, which:<br/><br/>(a) are contracting authorities or public undertakings and which pursue one of the activities referred to in Articles 8 to 14 of the Directive 2014/25/EU.<br/><br/>(b) when they are not contracting authorities or public undertakings, have as one of their activities any of the activities referred to in Articles 8 to 14, or any combination thereof and operate on the basis of special or exclusive rights granted by a competent authority of a Member State.<br/><br/>Additional Information<br/><br/>The indicator is needed in order to discriminate between those contracts where the Contracting Entity acts as a Contracting Authority undergoing the limits and the rules of Directive 24 and those where it acts as a  Contracting Entity, with certain relaxed constraints.<br/>For example, the Contracting Entities have different thresholds for the application of Directive 24 if compared with Contracting Authorities.<br/><br/>WG Approval 28/04/2020<br/><br/>

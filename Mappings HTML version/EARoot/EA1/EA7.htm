<li>One of the activities that takes place in the Procurement Procedure life-cycle is the evaluation of Tenderers and Tenders, and the awarding of a contract to one or more Tenderer. The awarded Tenderer(s) are the "Winner(s)".</li><li>The Contract may attach other Procurement Documents and other types of Documents.</li><li>The object of the Contract and additional data that where stated in the Procurement Project are also placed in the contract Purpose (e.g. Subject Matter, Place of Performance, Total Magnitude Quantity, etc.).</li><li>Similarly, the values of the Procurement that where initially estimated in the Procurement Project are set in the Procurement Value class.</li><li>The Contract reflects also the Awarding Results (resulting from the evaluation) and the signatory parties (Procuring Entities and Winners).</li><li>In case the Procurement Procedure uses Framework Agreement as Technique, the contract refers to it.</li></ol>

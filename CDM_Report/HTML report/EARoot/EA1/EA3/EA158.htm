Software solutions and electronic devices for communication and exchange of information between contracting authorities and economic operators.<br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/>These tools can be used for, at least, these five situations:<br/><br/>1. The URL where to access the procurement documents.<br/>2. Access point for the submission of tender documents.<br/>3. Download URL, to indicate where to get a tool to communicate with the CA<br/>4. The URI of the system used by a Technique to allow EOs to exchange information with the CA (e.g. eAuction and DPS Systems)<br/>5. Exchange of information between CA and EO for questions and clarifications<br/><br/>WG Approval 2019-02-05<br/>

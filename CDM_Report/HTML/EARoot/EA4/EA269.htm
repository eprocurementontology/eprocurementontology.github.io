Organisation that provides ancillary purchasing activities for other procuring entities.<br/><br/>WG Pending of approval<br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/><br/>Procurement Service Providers can also be other public administrations acting on behalf and for the account of the buyer, as explained in Directive 2014/24/EU, Article 2. (15) c (definition of 'ancillary activities'): <br/><br/>(c) preparation and management of procurement<br/>procedures on behalf and for the account of the<br/>contracting authority concerned;<br/><br/>This would be equivalent to the concept of 'procuringEntity' in OCDS: 'The entity managing the procurement, which may be different from the buyer who is paying / using the items being procured'.<br/><br/>

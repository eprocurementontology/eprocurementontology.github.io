Notes: The number of solutions or tenders will be reduced in iterative evaluations for multiple staged procedures.<br/><br/>(Pending to review by the WG)<br/><br/>Additional information:<br/>This refers to multiple-stage procedures (included two-stage procedures, at least). Open Procedures can be seen as one-stage procedures. In multiple-stage procedure the first stage (or round) is used for the selection of the candidates, whilst the rest of stages is about submitting tenders and warning (or not) about the right of the CA to award without further negotiations. See Article 29(4) of Directive 2014/24/EU. <br/>

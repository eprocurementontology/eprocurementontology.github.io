<li>The Procurement Terms are "conditions or stipulations established by the Procuring  Entity:</li></ol>
<ul>
<li><u>Procedure Terms</u>: conditions and stipulations determining how the procurement procedure is executed.</li><li><u>Review Terms</u>: conditions and stipulations about the information and organisation responsible for the revision of a Procurement Procedure.</li><li><u>Tender Submission Terms</u>: conditions and stipulations about the Tender and its submission.</li><li><u>Contract Terms</u>: conditions and stipulations related to the implementation of the contract.</li><li><u>Tender Evaluation Terms</u>: conditions and stipulations to evaluate the tenders.</li><li><u>Award Terms</u>: conditions and stipulations to determine how the procurement procedure is awarded.</li></ul>

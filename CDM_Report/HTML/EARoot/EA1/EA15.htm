<li>An Economic Operator is an organisation.</li><li>Economic Operators can be Tenderers (the submitter of the Tender) or sub-contractors.</li><li>When the Economic Operators are members of a group (e.g. Consortia, Joint ventures, Undertaking (EO Group Type)), and they play different roles, e.g. group lead entity, member of the group, etc. (EO Role Type).</li><li>The Winner of a contract is a tenderer or group of Tenderers.</li><li>Tenderers may rely on other Economic Operator that are subcontractors but not tenderers.</li><li>When guarantees are required by the Procuring Entity, Economic Operators may have to provide Financial Account details (e.g. a bank account data).</li></ol>
